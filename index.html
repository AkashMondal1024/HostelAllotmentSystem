Hostel Rules

"Hostel Rules are essential guidelines designed to maintain order, safety, and harmony within the hostel premises. These rules cover various aspects, including quiet hours, cleanliness, guest policies, and curfew timings. Adherence to these rules fosters a conducive environment for studying and living, promoting mutual respect and consideration among residents. Violations may result in disciplinary actions to ensure the well-being and comfort of all residents."

Accomodation Details

"Accommodation at our hostel provides a comfortable and secure living environment tailored to meet the needs of students. Rooms are allocated based on availability and preferences, ranging from single to shared accommodations. Each room is equipped with basic furniture and amenities, ensuring a functional and pleasant stay. Allocation is done fairly, with priority given to senior students and those with special needs, ensuring a balanced and inclusive residential community."

Facilties

"Our hostel offers a variety of facilities aimed at enhancing the living experience of residents. These include a well-maintained dining hall, laundry services, study rooms, common areas with recreational equipment, and high-speed internet access. Additionally, round-the-clock security and emergency services are in place to ensure safety. These facilities aim to create a comfortable, engaging, and productive environment for all residents."

Anti-Ragging

"Ragging in any form is strictly prohibited within the hostel and its premises. The hostel has a zero-tolerance policy towards ragging, and any such incidents will be met with serious disciplinary action, including expulsion if necessary. Residents are encouraged to report any instances of ragging to the hostel authorities immediately. We are committed to ensuring a safe, respectful, and supportive community for everyone."

Authorities

"The hostel is managed by a dedicated team of authorities, including the Hostel Warden, Resident Advisors, and Security Personnel. The Warden is responsible for overall hostel management, addressing student concerns, and ensuring compliance with hostel regulations. Resident Advisors are available to assist students with day-to-day needs and act as a liaison between students and the administration. Security personnel ensure the safety and security of residents at all times."
