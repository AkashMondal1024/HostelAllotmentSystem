Authorities

  • The Chief Warden oversees the overall administration and management of hostel facilities.
  • Each hostel block has a dedicated Warden responsible for student welfare and daily supervision.
  • Resident Tutors are appointed to assist students in academic and personal concerns.
  • The Hostel Office manages accommodation records, fee processing, and maintenance coordination.
  • Security officers ensure round-the-clock safety and enforce entry/exit regulations.
  • A dedicated Housekeeping Supervisor maintains hygiene standards in hostel premises.
  • The Mess Committee, involving student representatives, collaborates with the mess manager for food quality and menu planning.
  • The Anti-Ragging Committee includes faculty members and student representatives for monitoring and prevention.
  • Students can reach authorities through scheduled office hours or official communication channels.
  • Emergency contact numbers of key authorities are displayed at hostel notice boards and entry points.
