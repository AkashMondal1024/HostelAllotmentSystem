Hostel Rules

  • Students must return to the hostel by 9:00 PM.
  • Visitors are allowed only from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM.
  • Silence must be maintained after 10:00 PM.
  • Keep the premises clean and hygienic.
  • Damaging property will lead to disciplinary action.
  • Ragging is strictly prohibited and punishable by law.
  • All students must inform the warden before leaving overnight.
  • Students must carry their hostel ID cards at all times and present them when asked by authorities.
  • Lights and fans must be switched off when leaving the room to conserve electricity.
  • Personal celebrations or gatherings inside rooms require prior approval from the warden.
  • No unauthorized person is allowed to stay overnight in the hostel.
  • Any form of theft, including borrowing without permission, will result in strict action.
