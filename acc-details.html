Accommodation Details

  • Each room is shared by two students and is equipped with two beds, study tables, chairs, and cupboards.
  • Hostel floors are segregated year-wise to ensure a comfortable and familiar environment.
  • Common washrooms and drinking water facilities are available on every floor.
  • Wi-Fi is available in all hostel areas with fair usage limits.
  • Each hostel has a common room with TV, newspapers, indoor games, and space for group studies.
  • 24/7 security is provided with entry monitored via ID cards and biometric scans.
  • Mess services provide vegetarian and non-vegetarian options with a rotating menu.
  • Dedicated laundry areas and ironing stations are available inside the hostel premises.
  • Power backup is available for essential lights and fans during outages.
