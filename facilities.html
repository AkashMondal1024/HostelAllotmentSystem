Hostel Facilities

  • Fully furnished rooms with cots, study tables, chairs, and individual wardrobes.

  • High-speed Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the hostel premises.

  • Dedicated maintenance team for electrical, plumbing, and housekeeping services.
  • In-campus stationary and utility shop for basic student needs.
